Get Out of Control!

"Get Out Of Control!" is a calm puzzle game where you help an adorable tiny monster get out of the Ctrl key, where you modify the level structure by pressing the actual Ctrl key in your keyboard.

This game was made with Unity by Marcos Agudo (@mercadodev13) and me for the GMTK JAM 2020 in under 48 hours. The theme of the jam was "Out of Control" and we took the concept literally in order to make a puzzle game.

I was in charge of translating the idea into a game concept and designing the 8 levels that make it up. I also worked as a 3D artist for the character and the final levels.

For being our second Game Jam, and my first approach in designing puzzle games, it turned out really well, ranking #391 out of 5000+ submissions. Some of the reviews we got are:
