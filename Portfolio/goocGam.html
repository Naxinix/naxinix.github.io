Get Out of Control!



A calm puzzle game where you help an adorable tiny monster escape from a CTRL-key-shaped maze, by actually pressing the Ctrl key in your keyboard





Overview and Inspiration

  • This game was made with Unity by Marcos Agudo (@mercadodev13) and me for the GMTK JAM 2020 in under 48 hours. The theme of the jam was "Out of Control" and we took the concept literally in order to make a puzzle game.

  • Given the short time, we decided to focus on a single core mechanic and see how far we could push it in a reasonable amount of levels.

  • We also wanted the player to know what to do just by looking at the level, but let him figure out how to do it. That's why we aimed for a simple but clear style and a god-like view from the camera.

  • My responsibilities were:
    • Translate the idea into a game concept
    • Design and prototype the 8 levels that make the game up
    • Model the playable character and the final levels
    • Work on the sound effects
