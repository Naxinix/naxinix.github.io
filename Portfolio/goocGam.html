Roles: Game Designer, Level Designer, 3D Artist, Sound Designer
Platform: Windows
Engine: Unity

What is it?

"Get Out Of Control!" is a calm puzzle game where you help an adorable tiny monster get out of a CTRL-key-shaped maze, in which the level structure changes by pressing the actual Ctrl key in your keyboard.

This game was made with Unity by Marcos Agudo (@mercadodev13) and me for the GMTK JAM 2020 in under 48 hours. The theme of the jam was "Out of Control" and we took the concept literally in order to make a puzzle game.

What did I do in it?

  • Translated the idea into a game concept
  • Designed and prototyped the 8 levels that make the game up
  • Modelled the playable character and the final levels
  • Worked on the sound effects
