Japanese Environment

As the acclaimed "Ghost of Tsushima" came out, I made this scene in Unreal Engine 4 as a fan tribute to the game.

Greatly inspired by the game's scenery, the result is currently in development to become a whole playable level, based on the layouts, tone, pacing and subject matter of the real game.

Assets used: Infinity Blade: Temple
