Blocktober 2020

Starting in 2017, Blocktober is a trend in which every October, Level Designers from all over the world share their level blockouts on Twitter as a form of art.
I decided to take part on it this year, doing some quick level concepts along the month using Unreal Engine, each on them inspired by a specific theme.

Military Base

Under the theme Military Base, I made a concept level in which you have to infiltrate in a square-shaped base without getting caught by the guards on each corner.

Gothic Castle

The theme this time was Gothic Castle. For the blockout I tried to use simple pointed shapes without too much detail, to give it the Gothic feeling. The castle itself has 4 floors and 2 corridors that connect with the structures on each side.
