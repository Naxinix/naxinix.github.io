Inspiration and Preproduction

As a huge fan of The Last of Us games, as well as their level design and scenery, I decided to make a level for its beloved multiplayer mode. In "The Last Of Us Online", two teams fight for supplies. So far, there are 4 gamemodes. The maps are viable for all gamemodes and they don't rely on level structure istelf but on the objectives in it.

Far from other frenetic shooter games, a unique feature of TLOU Online is the importance of stealth, so the maps are covered with props and structures to avoid being seen.
