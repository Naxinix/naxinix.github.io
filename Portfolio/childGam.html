What is it?

Child Of My Eyes is a frenetic boss rush with bullet hell influences, where you face the internal duels that flourish after the loss of a loved one.

This game was made with Unity by a team of 5 for the Spain Game Devs Jam II, which lasted one week. The theme of the jam was "Duel" and we decided to take KÃ¼bler-Ross model and turn the stages of grief into bosses that represent these stages.

What did I do in it?

  • Designed the bosses concepts and their attacks
  • Made all the 2D art
  • Made all the animations of the game
  • Worked on the sound effects

Creative Process

  • The whole starting idea was to make a game overall difficult, in which you have to learn the boss attack patterns in order to succeed, while avoiding being too frustrating.
  • Moreover, each boss had to be designed in a way that represented a stage of grief not only through their appearance and animations, but also in their behaviour and attacks. For instance, while "Depression" is a passive boss with slow but constant projectiles, "Wrath" is about fast projectiles that make you move frenetically.
  • Another imporant aspect to consider during the design process was to make the bosses individual attacks have synergy between each other. This way, the game flow will smoothly and the animation would transition smoothly
