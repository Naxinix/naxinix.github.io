Child Of My Eyes

Child Of My Eyes is a boss rush with bullet hell influences, where you face the internal duels that flourish after the loss of a loved one.

This game was made with Unity by a team of 5 for the Spain Game Devs Jam II, which lasted one week. The theme of the jam was "Duel" and we decided to take KÃ¼bler-Ross model and turn the stages of grief into bosses that represent these stages.

I was in charge of designing the bosses mechanics and their attacks. The idea was to make a game overall difficult, in which you have to learn the boss attack patterns in order to succeed, while avoiding being too frustrating. Moreover, each boss had to be designed in a way that represented a stage of grief not only visually, but also in their behaviour. For instance, while "Depression" is a passive boss with slow but constant projectiles, "Wrath" is about fast projectiles that make you move frenetically.

In addition, it is worth mentioning that I also worked as the 2D artist and animator for the 4 bosses that make up the game.
