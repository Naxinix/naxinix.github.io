Traffic Jammer is a frenetic mobile game about a novice traffic guard that has to guide fast approaching cars through their desired destination.

Traffic Jammer is intended to be a super casual mobile game, and for that reason que core loop and controls are very simple and easy to learn.

Red and blue cars will come out of grey tunnels and will turn in the direction you (the traffic guard) are pointing when they reach the final bifurcation. Your mission is slide the finger in the direction you want to point, so each incoming car matches its destination tunnel color. As the game progresses, the difficulty curve rises, and so the cars will move faster and more roads will open! How rounds will you last?

I’m making this game in Unity and on my own, for learning purposes. Therefore, I’m taking the roles of designer, programmer and 3D artist.
